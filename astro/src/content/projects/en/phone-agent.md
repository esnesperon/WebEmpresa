Phone Agent is an artificial intelligence agent that answers phone calls autonomously. Configured with business information, it can handle queries about schedules, services, availability and any information programmed into it.

## Capabilities

- **Call reception**: Answers incoming calls with natural AI-generated voice
- **Contextual understanding**: Understands the caller's intent and extracts relevant information from the conversation
- **Knowledge base**: Queries a configured database with business information (services, prices, schedules, FAQ)
- **Smart transfer**: Routes to a human when it detects that the query requires personal intervention
- **Call logs**: Stores transcriptions, summaries and metrics for every call
- **Available 24/7**: No breaks needed, handles calls outside business hours

## Applications

- After-hours customer service
- Appointment booking and availability queries
- Information about services and pricing
- Call filtering and lead capture
- Internal support for sales teams

## Technology

- Real-time voice processing (STT + TTS)
- LLM with RAG for knowledge base queries
- VoIP PBX integration
- Metrics and transcription dashboard
